Tanzania welcomes you to visit and enjoy the tourist attractions including national parks, mountains and cultural heritage of masai people. Tanzania is located in east Africa south of Kenya. It is the home of Mountain Kilimanjoro the highest peak in Africa. The home of the largest national game reserve know as Serengeti national park. It is the motherland of Swahili language and the diversity of culture in Tanzania makes it a perfect gateway place. Karibu Tanzania (Welcome to Tanzania).

The giant standing at 19,340 feet high, Mountain Kilimanjaro is the highest in africa. Its between the two city of Arusha and Moshi.
